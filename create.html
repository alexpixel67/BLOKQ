What is RTP?
RTP (Return-To-Player) is the total percentage of wagered money mathematically paid back to players over time.

If your RTP is 90%, for every 100 BQ wagered, players win back 90 BQ, and you (the creator) keep 10 BQ as profit (The House Edge).

Note: The "Total Chance" must equal exactly 100%, and RTP cannot exceed 99% to prevent economy draining.
